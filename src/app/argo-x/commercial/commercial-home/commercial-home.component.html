OUR EXCLUSIVE SERVICES

Marine vessel is designed for multi-mission in various fields; military, civil defense, and commercial with customized instruments and specifications.

Speed

The 12-meter-long marine vessel has a maximum speed of 60kn which perfectly can conduct rescue missions in different water environments. It’s also equipped with autonomous obstacle avoidance & autonomous path planning and a 3D-colored underwater sonar with two transducers and three frequency bands.

Gyroscopic Stabilisation

The installed Gyroscopic stabilizer ensures stability in rough water and offshore platforms in the event of a rapid need for any of the supplies or tools. It’s supported by a weather station to measure wind, temperature, pressure, location, turn rate, acceleration, and heading. Also, the vessel design is multistep capsulated to stand in high waves. It’s significant for firing missiles for military purposes.

Tracking & Monitoring

The deployed marine radar and AIS for surveillance, tracking and, identification are a gazing eye collecting data and composed of visual (Electro-Optical), thermal, laser multi sensory surveillance, night camera, and movable searchlights. Additionally, It has a high communication bandwidth equipment with up to 80-100 km range.

Emergency Kit

TOPHAN saves lives as it’s equipped with essential medical and emergency kits for fast medical services.
